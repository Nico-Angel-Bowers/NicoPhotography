About

Who I Am

I am passionate about creating a space for freedom of expression. Based in Exeter, New Hampshire, I am dedicated to providing an exceptional website to host photos and creations that meet the needs of my customers. With a commitment to quality and innovation, I aim to inspire and engage with everyone who visits my site.

My Story

I was born in New York City, above my parent’s restaurant Corner Social. I founded this website in 2016 with the vision of a way to share photography in a professional way.

My Values

  • Quality: I strive for excellence in everything I do.
  • Customer Focus: My customers are at the heart of my work.
  • Sustainability: I am committed to making environmentally responsible choices.
